# Privacy Policy

**Revise It | Last updated: April 2026**

## 1. Who we are

Revise It is an educational technology company based in South Africa. We build assessment tools for teachers and departments in the FET phase.

**Responsible party:** Kahueka Huntley
**Contact:** kahueka@reviseit.io

---

## 2. What information we collect

When you request our catalogue or apply to join our pilot, we collect:

- Your name and email address
- Your cellphone number (pilot form only)
- Your school name and role
- Your department's subject area, grade levels, and size
- Your responses to open-ended questions about your challenges and goals

---

## 3. Why we collect it

We use this information solely to:

- Send you the requested catalogue
- Contact you to discuss the pilot programme
- Understand your context so we can serve you better

We will not use your information for unrelated marketing without your explicit consent.

---

## 4. How we store it

Your information is stored securely using Tally (our form provider). We do not sell your data to third parties.

---

## 5. How long we keep it

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or until you request deletion.

---

## 6. Your rights under POPIA

You have the right to:

- Access the information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at kahueka@reviseit.io.

---

## 7. Complaints

If you believe we have handled your information unlawfully, you may lodge a complaint with the **Information Regulator of South Africa** at [www.inforegulator.org.za](https://www.inforegulator.org.za).
